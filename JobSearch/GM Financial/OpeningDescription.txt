About The Role

The Software Development Engineer (SDE) contributes in all areas of development as a member of an 
Agile Scrum team. Team members will have areas of strength and some level of specialization, 
but must be adept at learning tools and languages that enable the team member to participate 
in all areas of product development. Being adaptable and knowledgeable in multiple domains 
enables the team members to swarm or fill gaps in order to meet commitments.
